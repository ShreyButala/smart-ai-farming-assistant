# 🌾 Smart AI Farming Assistant  

## 📌 Overview  
Farmers face unpredictable weather, wrong crop selection, language barriers, and limited access to expert advice.  
**Smart AI Farming Assistant** is an AI-powered platform designed to empower farmers with:  

- 🌧️ **Weather prediction**  
- 🌱 **Crop recommendation**  
- 🌿 **Plant disease detection**  
- 🗣️ **Multilingual chatbot** (Hindi & Gujarati)  
- 🏛️ **Government scheme guidance**  
- 🤝 **Community Q&A forum**  

It works on **smartphones and even basic phones** (via SMS/IVR), ensuring accessibility for all.  

---

## 🚜 Problem We Solve  
- 🌧️ Unpredictable weather → crop failures and losses  
- 🌱 Wrong crop selection → reduced yield  
- 🗣️ Language barriers → farmers can’t use English-based apps  
- 🏷️ Lack of info → no clarity on schemes or market prices  
- 🌿 Crop disease identification → difficult without experts  
- 📶 Poor internet → need for offline/SMS solutions  

---

## 💡 Our Solution  
✔ **AI Weather & Crop Prediction** → forecasts rainfall & temperature, suggests crops  
✔ **Multilingual Chatbot** → voice/text advice in Hindi & Gujarati  
✔ **Plant Disease Detection** → upload leaf photo → AI suggests remedies  
✔ **Government Scheme Guidance** → easy-to-understand benefits & process  
✔ **Farmer Community Forum** → peer-to-peer learning  
✔ **Offline & SMS/IVR Support** → works even with poor connectivity  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack  

- **Frontend** → HTML, CSS, JavaScript  
- **AI/ML** → Weather prediction, Crop recommendation, Plant disease models  
- **APIs** → OpenWeatherMap, Plant.id / Hugging Face, Govt. portals  
- **Communication** → SMS/IVR (Twilio/Exotel)  
- **Storage** → LocalStorage (offline) / Cloud (future)  

---

## 🖥️ How It Works  

1. 🌐 **Farmer chooses language** (Homepage).  
2. 📊 **Weather & soil data collected** (via APIs or farmer input).  
3. 🌱 **AI recommends crops** + planting tips.  
4. 🤖 **Chatbot guides** on watering, fertilizers, schemes.  
5. 📷 **Farmer uploads crop image** → AI detects disease.  
6. 📞 **Offline/SMS fallback** → ensures access without internet.
